## Coca-Cola-USA-Retailer-Data-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard creation using MS Excel)
## Project Objective
To transform raw Coca-Cola USA of 2022 & 2023 sales data into meaningful insights using Excel and deliver a user-friendly interactive dashboard.

## Dataset used
- <a href="https://github.com/kopparthiLokesh/Coca-Cola-USA-Retailer-Data-analysis/blob/main/Retailer%20Dataset.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
-What is the total sales, units sold, average price, and operating profit?
-How do total sales compare between 2022 and 2023?
-Which beverage brand generates the highest sales?
-Which retailer contributes the most revenue?
-Which brands show the highest year-over-year growth?
-Which retailers have the largest positive sales variance?
-Which quarter records the highest sales?
-What is the average operating profit margin by quarter?

## Process
-Collected and cleaned Coca-Cola USA sales data by removing duplicates, fixing errors, and standardizing formats.
-Transformed data by creating calculated fields and grouping it by year, quarter, brand, retailer, and region.
-Analyzed the data to build key KPIs such as total sales, units sold, operating profit, and year-over-year variance.
-Designed an interactive dashboard with charts, KPI cards, and slicers to present insights clearly.

## Project Insight
-Total sales and operating profit increased significantly in 2023 compared to 2022.
-Coca-Cola and Dasani Water are the top-performing beverage brands by sales.
-Amazon and FizzyCo contribute the highest share of retailer sales.
-Q3 2023 recorded the highest sales and profit margin among all quarters.

## Final Conclusion:
The interactive dashboard converts raw Coca-Cola USA sales data into clear insights, helping identify top brands, retailers, and growth opportunities for better decision-making.
